Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how WABridge handles your data.

1. Introduction

WABridge ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our WhatsApp Business API integration services.

We are based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, and comply with applicable data protection laws including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) of India and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for our EU users.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, business name, and billing details when you register for our services.
  • Contact Form Data: Information submitted through our website contact forms.
  • WhatsApp Business Data: Phone numbers, message content, and conversation history processed through our platform on your behalf.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, and interaction patterns.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers.
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication and analytics cookies for service improvement.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for:

  • Providing and maintaining our WhatsApp Business API services
  • Processing and delivering messages on your behalf
  • Sending service-related communications and updates
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries
  • Improving our services and developing new features
  • Ensuring security and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. WhatsApp Business Data Processing

As a WhatsApp Business Solution Provider, we process messages and contacts on behalf of our business clients. This processing is governed by:

  • Meta's Business Terms: We comply with Meta/Facebook's policies for WhatsApp Business API usage.
  • Data Processor Role: We act as a data processor; our clients (businesses) are the data controllers for their customer data.
  • Message Retention: Messages are retained only as long as necessary for service delivery and as required by law.
  • End-to-End Encryption: WhatsApp messages are encrypted in transit as per WhatsApp's security standards.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information only with:

  • Meta/WhatsApp: To facilitate WhatsApp Business API services
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (AWS, Google Cloud), payment processors, and analytics providers under strict data protection agreements
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for data at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Employee data protection training

7. Your Rights

7.1 Under DPDP Act (India)

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure of data
  • Right to grievance redressal
  • Right to nominate

7.2 Under GDPR (EU Users)

  • Right to access, rectification, and erasure
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

8. Opt-Out Options

You may opt out of:

  • Marketing Communications: Click "unsubscribe" in emails or contact us
  • Cookies: Adjust browser settings or use our cookie preference center
  • Data Processing: Request account deletion by emailing privacy@wabridge.in

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for:

  • Account Data: As long as your account is active
  • Message Data: Up to 90 days or as required by client contracts
  • Billing Records: 7 years as per Indian tax regulations
  • Analytics Data: 24 months in anonymized form

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in servers located outside India. We ensure appropriate safeguards including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers
  • Data processing agreements with all service providers
  • Compliance with cross-border data transfer requirements

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us for immediate deletion.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be notified via email or website notice. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

  • Address: WABridge, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India
